About me Software engineer in training

My name is Tatiana Tassi and I am 24 years old. I was born and grew up in Italy, in a small town near Bologna. I studied for a few years at the Faculty of Foreign Languages and Literatures in Bologna, and in 2011 I moved to Jyväskylä, Finland, with the Erasmus programme. As an exchange student I improved my knowledge of Finnish language, literature and culture. In 2013 I abandoned the studies in Bologna, got married and started my degree studies in Finland.

I am currently studying information technology in Metropolia UAS, Helsinki, specializing in software engineering. After graduation I plan to proceed with my studies to a Master's degree in computer science.

For what concerns hobbies I like reading, drawing, PC gaming, crocheting, improving my proficiency in Finnish language, and listening to music. My favourite bands include Siouxie and the Banshees and the Sisters of Mercy, while my favourite authors are J.R.R. Tolkien and H.P.Lovecraft. I am obsessed with anything related to Alice in Wonderland. My husband is a Finnish student of computer science named Heikki; we do not have children, but we have an almost-2-years-old godson, Taisto. We also have two cats, Metku and Teho, a dog, Peikko, and a small colony of pet mice.
